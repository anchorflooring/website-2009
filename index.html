About Us

Based in Lancashire, Anchor Flooring Ltd has been established since 1995 and has been supplying flooring services to the Health service, Local authorities, Education department, Universities, Hotel and hospitality sector, contract and commercial industry for nearly 15 years.

Our Work

We have carried out a wide range of work, from general repairs to £100,000 national contracts. Our services include:

  • Uplift & Removal of Existing Flooring
  • Application of Floor Primers
  • Supply and Installation
  • Vinyl Flooring
  • Sheet Carpets
  • Floor Tiling
